Virus Protection

Our sytem is built to protect every document from viruses and unauthorized tampering.

Features:

  1. Document storage and management on a decentralised, secure and tamper-proof blockchain network.
  2. Digital signatures and authentication to ensure the integrity and authenticity of documents.
  3. Traceability and auditability of document transactions and changes.
  4. Access control and permission management to determine who can view, edit or share documents.
  5. Integration with existing document management sytem and workflows.

Benefits:

  1. Improved security and privacy of sensitive documents.
  2. Reduced risk of document tampering, fraud and data breaches.
  3. Enhanced transparency and accountability in document transactions.
  4. Increased efficiency and speed in document processing and sharing.
  5. Improved collaboration and communication among stakeholders.
